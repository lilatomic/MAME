WHO ARE WE ?

The McGill Association of Mechanical Engineers (MAME) is the voice of the students. The student council of the association provides yearlong services and events for all of the students within the mechanical department. We aim to support you academically, professionally, and socially. In terms of academics, we voice all concerns and or suggestions to the department in order to enrich your McGill undergraduate studies. Building networks is also a big part of our goal, such that as you have as many opportunities as possible upon your graduation. Moreover, MAME also strives to maintain a fun and exciting atmosphere throughout the year by creating many social events in addition to the academic ones. These range from weekend long CAD competitions to holiday dinners.

University is challenging. It will also be one of the best experiences in your life. MAME’s main purpose is to improve this experience for all mechanical engineering students; so take advantage of it! Have any questions, contact us any time or stop by our office (MC120) any day from 10am - 4pm.
