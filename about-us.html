Dimitri Calomiris

President

Contact Email:mame.president@mcgilleus.ca

Description of Position: The President of MAME acts as a liaison between MAME and the Mechanical Engineering department, the EUS, and other groups on campus. The president also guides the MAME council rock the jobs you elected them to do in addition to the small tasks that are taken care of behind the scenes. If ever a mechie is in need of anything, the President is always a good source to get help!

A bit about Myself: Heya! My name is Dimitri (many people call me Dimi for short) and I’m a die hard MAMEr. I’ve been involved helping mech students since my first week at McGill. This is my fourth year working for MAME and I’m excited to continue improving student life for the mechies. As for my personal life, I love grabbing a beer and chatting with people, especially at OAP (#theBestPlaceOnEarth). I’m also an avid video game player; always down to chat about some good old fashion games. I pretty much live in MC120, so come by anytime to say hello!

Favourite MAME-ory: It may not be my favorite, but hell it’s one famous story by now. When I was in my first year, and had no idea what E-Week really was, I lost our team’s castle piece. It was a tragic moment. My team was mad. We bonded. Looking back, it was a stepping stone into an amazing time in engineering. I’ll never forget it.

My Hidden Talent: I was a top ranking video game player for a while.
